Front End Engineer 

 

For more than one hundred years, IBM has been helping governments, businesses, and other organisations all over the world leverage technology to drive and implement change and to promote and accelerate scientific discovery. Along the way, we and the world have weathered many storms. And each time IBM has emerged a stronger, more capable company. 

To continue to help the clients we support achieve their goals, IBM recently acquired Envizi. 

Envizi is a global leader in energy and sustainability data and analytics software that enables reporting and management of ESG indicators including energy, greenhouse gases, environmental and social responsibility metrics. They are already helping companies all over the globe reach their sustainability goals and drive better business outcomes.

 

What are we looking for?

 

We are looking for a talented and passionate Front-End Engineer to join our growing engineering team to develop and deliver reactive web experiences for our market-leading product. 

As a Front-End Developer, you will be responsible for delivering the front-end capability of the platform across the whole SDLC and is a great opportunity contribute to the innovation of our platform. In this role you will work closely with Product Managers, UX Designers, QA, and Engineers.

At Envizi we believe there is a smarter way to achieve a sustainable future, and we are helping organizations harness the power of data and analytics to optimize for a low carbon future. 

 

What will be my key responsibilities?

  • Collaborate with Product Managers, UX Designers, Engineers and QA to design, develop and deploy new platform features and functionality for our SaaS product 
  • Contribute to the design and build evolution of the platform, ensuring it remains scalable, reliable, and easy to use. 
  • Translate designs and wireframes into high-quality, responsive user interface components, while adhering to the defined requirements and specifications 
  • Uphold quality standards including reliability, efficiency, security, maintainability and usability by applying best practice processes, methodologies and tools 
  • Contribute to engineering discussions across our platform

 

What skills and experience will I need?

 

  • Experience in front-end development with strong skills in JavaScript, CSS, HTML5, TypeScript
  • 3+ years experience working with Angular (version 8+) 
  • Ability to write high-performance, reusable code, including appropriate testing 
  • Cross-browser compatibility experience with all major browsers, operating systems and device types 
  • Understanding of DevOps, CI/CD tools, and concepts 
  • End to end project build experience with post-implementation support

 

What skills and experience are ideal to have?

 

  • Experience working with high traffic web application 
  • Experience on unit testing tools like Karma or Jasmine 
  • Experience developing custom directive and components 
  • Experience on working with secure backend APIs
  • Knowledge and experience with code versioning tools 
  • Experience working on UI/UX design system like Carbon Design, Ant Design or Material Design

 

You must be a forward-thinking, proactive team player with excellent communication skills. 

You must be motivated, passionate and a creative problem-solver with strong attention to detail.

 

This is an exciting opportunity to work with a dynamic, entrepreneurial team in one of the fastest growing sectors of the "green economy".

A global leader in energy and sustainability data and analytics software that enables reporting and management of ESG indicators including energy, greenhouse gases, environmental and social responsibility metrics. 

You will be applying for one of the most exciting opportunities where technology is being used to help drive global impact & change while working with a dynamic and entrepreneurial team in one of the fastest growing sectors of the "green economy”.

Sign me up!

If you tick these boxes, and are ready to start your next challenge with a career at IBM – click “Apply” today. To find out more, head to www.ibm.com.au/careers
